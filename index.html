What We Learned

Lessons Learned

Pragmatic lessons across technology, organisation, and risk & governance.

Practical Takeaways

Technology

⬡

Start small, opinionated

Pick a tight default stack; reduce cognitive load and onboarding time.

◆

Avoid platform sprawl

Fewer platforms, clear ownership, and explicit deprecation paths.

◈

Build reusability

Invest in platforms and patternising (templates, libraries, reference apps).

Organisation + Governance

◎

Business + IT together

Close collaboration from problem framing to operations; shared KPIs.

✦

Incentivise reuse

Reward teams for adopting common components rather than bespoke builds.

✓

Governance as a carrot

Balance innovation with risk; educate users on responsibilities and imperfections.

Closing Note
